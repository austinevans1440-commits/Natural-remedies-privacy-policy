Privacy Policy

Natural Remedies for iOS

Effective March 6, 2026 Your privacy matters

Overview

Natural Remedies is an educational reference app that provides information about herbs, supplements, essential oils, and natural wellness practices. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information — or more accurately, how we don't collect it.

Natural Remedies does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Your privacy is fully protected.

No Personal Data Collection

We do not collect any personal information whatsoever. The app does not ask for your name, email address, location, or any other identifying information. No account creation or registration is required to use Natural Remedies.

Local-Only Storage

When you save favorites or bookmarks within the app, this data is stored exclusively on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This information never leaves your device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third-party service.

If you delete the app, all locally stored data is permanently removed from your device.

Third-Party Links

Natural Remedies contains links to iHerb.com, a third-party retailer, through an affiliate partnership. When you tap one of these links, you will leave the Natural Remedies app and be directed to the iHerb website or app.

Once you leave Natural Remedies, your activity is governed by iHerb's own privacy policy. We encourage you to review their privacy practices before providing any information to third-party sites.

We receive no personal data about you from iHerb or any other third party.

No Analytics or Tracking

Natural Remedies does not use any third-party analytics services, tracking pixels, advertising SDKs, or ad networks. We do not track your usage patterns, browsing behavior, or any other activity within the app.

No Cookies

As a native iOS application, Natural Remedies does not use cookies or any similar web-based tracking technologies.

Children's Privacy

Natural Remedies is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. Since the app collects no personal data from any user, there is no risk of inadvertent collection of children's information.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. Since we do not collect your contact information, we are unable to notify you directly of changes. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
